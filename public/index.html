GenICNS

Generate ICNS and ICO files for your desktop apps.

Download for macOS

What is GenICNS?

GenICNS is a GUI wrapper for png2icons: awesome Node.js module that generates Apple ICNS and Microsoft ICO files from PNG files.

You have to only drop your PNG files to get multiple-sized ICO and ICNS files.

The ideal input is a 24 bit PNG with an alpha channel (RGBA) with 1024×1024 pixels but any other dimensions and most other PNG formats do also work.

If you only need to create ICO files 256×256 pixels are sufficient.

Privacy Policy

  • GenICNS and the developer do not collect the information of files opened by GenICNS.
  • GenICNS and the developer do not collect any personal information or privacy-related information about users.
